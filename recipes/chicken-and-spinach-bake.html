Chicken and Spinach Bake

A creamy baked chicken dish layered with fresh spinach and melted cheese.

This recipe begins by seasoning and saut√©ing diced chicken until it becomes golden and cooked through. Fresh spinach is then lightly wilted to preserve its bright green color and tender texture. Both ingredients are combined in a baking dish to prepare for the final step.

A generous layer of cheese is added on top before baking to create a creamy, bubbling finish. The result is a warm, comforting meal that pairs perfectly with rice, pasta, or crusty bread, making it ideal for a quick and satisfying dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, diced
  • Fresh spinach
  • Shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Season and cook the chicken until golden.
  2. Wilt the spinach in a pan until soft.
  3. Combine chicken and spinach in a baking dish.
  4. Cover with shredded cheese.
  5. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
